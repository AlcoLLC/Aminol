ABOUT AMINOL

How we started

The modernization of industry and renewal of equipment have increased demand for high-quality lubricants in Azerbaijan. To meet this, AMINOL Lubricants opened on December 15, 2017, in Sumgayit Chemical Industrial Park with a $20 million investment. The plant produces 150,000 tons annually, focusing on motor, hydraulic, transmission, compressor, turbine, and marine oils, along with antifreeze and AdBlue.

The power of innovation

AMINOL Lubricants ensures competitiveness with ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 17025, API, and ATIEL certifications. To boost exports, it secured approvals from major brands like Mercedes-Benz, General Electric, Volvo, Mack, and Renault. Benefiting from tax cuts in Sumgayit Chemical Industrial Park, it produces high-quality, eco-friendly products at competitive prices, delivering globally with a door-to-door approach.

Trusted Specialist

AMINOL Lubricants produces and packages high-quality oils using premium imported base oils and additives, formulated by expert engineers. A strict quality assurance policy ensures compliance with international standards. AMINOL Lubricants enhances engine and equipment performance with reliable starts, improved fuel economy, sustainability, and extended longevity through superior corrosion resistance.

PRODUCTION

Manufacturing processes

Aminol has the largest blender in the Caucasus region with an installed capacity of approximately 100 000 metric tonnes. The equipment used for manufacutring is sourced from well known international companies based in Europe, with proven track records in field operation. All manufacuring processes are automatically controlled and involve minimal outside involvement, which contributes to the homogeneity and stability of our product. The plant was projected, built and comissioned by a team of European engineers with deep experience in comissioning blending facilities in Europe and the CIS.

Our capacity

The plant includes within it a facility for blowmoulding plastic containers for oil and other deionised fluids blending facility, as well as storage warehouses, recreational area and an office building. At full capacity the plant can store over 40,000 metric tonnes of base oil in stationary tanks, and 8,000 metric tonnes in mobile railway tankers. The plant is an occupant of the Sumgait Chemical Industrial Park, located in the Republic of Azerbaijan. It is based on a total of 4,0 hectares of land. With its own railway line, and bulk loading and unloading equipment, the plant fulfills all the requirements of an in-land terminal.

SUSTAINABILITY

As a leading manufacturer of high-quality lubricants, we are committed to delivering solutions that align with global environmental standards and customer needs. This commitment drives our collaborations and investments in eco-friendly technologies and practices.

Advanced Additive Solutions

Aminol partners with global leaders in additive technology, such as Lubrizol, Chevron, Infenium, and Afton to integrate advanced low-SAPS (Sulphated Ash, Phosphorus, and Sulfur) additive packages into our formulations. These solutions are designed to reduce emissions, enhance fuel efficiency, and protect modern engines, meeting the demands of increasingly stringent environmental regulations.

Research and Development

Beyond working with industry-leading additive suppliers, Aminol invests heavily in its research and development (R&D) programs to pioneer eco-friendly innovations. Our R&D initiatives focus on: Creating formulations tailored for hybrid and electric vehicles, as well as high-performance engines. Developing longer-lasting lubricants to reduce waste and minimize environmental impact. Exploring renewable and biodegradable components to advance sustainability in every stage of production.

Our Vision for the Future

Sustainability is embedded in Aminol’s DNA. Through collaborative efforts, advanced R&D, and a commitment to innovation, we aim to lead the lubricant industry into a cleaner, greener future. Every product we deliver reflects our dedication to minimizing environmental impact while maximizing performance and reliability.

Commitment to Excellence

Aminol’s close partnerships with the main global additive manufacturers allow us to stay ahead of the curve, incorporating the latest advancements in additive technology. By leveraging these innovations, we provide high-performance solutions that meet both the environmental and operational needs of our customers.
